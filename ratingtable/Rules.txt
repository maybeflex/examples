Premier league rules of ranking regulations:
If any clubs finish with the same number of points,
their position in the Premier League table is determined by goal difference,
then the number of goals scored.
If the teams still cannot be separated, they will be awarded the same position in the table.

Seria A rules of ranking regulations:
If two or more teams are tied in points (for any place), the deciding tie-breakers are as follows:
1. Head-to-head records (results and points)
2. Goal difference of head-to-head games
3. Goal difference overall
4. Higher number of goals scored
